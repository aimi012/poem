Every day Poem:

On a cloudy day, The sky is so grey. We think of the peace That we could not keep. The world turns around But we are so sound. Our hopes unrealized And our dreams denied. We look up at the sky And realize how high We have set our sights, But nothing is right. Still, through every storm We should never forget That life can be beautiful And there's always hope to get.
